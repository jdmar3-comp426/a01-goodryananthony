There was once an Egyptian god so great that all crocodiles were revered simply because the god had the head of a crocodile.

Their respect for the god was so great that they mummified hundreds of crocodiles in the same linens they used for their greatest leaders.

The god in question is pictured below. Sobek, the god of crocodiles, was often depicted as either having the head of a crocodile or completely taking the form of a crocodile.
